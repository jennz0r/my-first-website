Some things you should know about Daleks

  • They hate and will kill every living being except Daleks.
  • Their only (slightly) weak spot is their eye.

  • Daleks believe that, You are superior (to Daleks) in only one respect - you are better at dying.

Some things you should know about cats

  • They hate and will kill every living being except cats.
  • Although cunning and homicidal, they love to LOL and will happily accept cheeseburgers in return for your life.
  • Cats can run at 30 miles per hour and purr at 26 purrs per second.
